Global Statistics

In this visualization, the aim is to create a compelling and informative representation of global statistics,
covering a wide range of key indicators such as the Consumer Price Index (CPI), Life Expectancy and more.
This visualization data is obtained from kaggle and the dataset has all the information about the global statistics.

______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Average Tax Revenue for each Continent

This bar chart shows the average tax revenue for each country in each continent.

Infant Mortality Rate vs Life Expectancy

This scatter plot is about the infant mortality rate against life expectancy around the world.

Percentage Of Language Spoken as Official Language In The World

This pie chart shows the number of the types of language spoken as an official language around the world

Unemployment rate of Primary education vs Tertiary education

This bubble chart shows the unemployment rate of primary education vs tertairy education.
The size of the bubble indicates the unemployment rate. The bigger the size, the higher the unemployment rate.

Global CPI Trends

This map chart is about the CPI value around the world. As you can see Iran has the highest CPI value.

Box Plot for Birth Rates by Continent

This box plot shows the birth rates by each country in each continent.
